7-Day Healthy and Balanced Meal Plan

Day 1 - Balanced Energy

  • Breakfast: Oatmeal with fresh berries and almonds
  • Snack: Greek yogurt with honey and walnuts
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken salad with mixed greens and vinaigrette
  • Snack: Apple slices with peanut butter
  • Dinner: Baked salmon with quinoa and steamed broccoli

Day 2 - High-Protein Focus

  • Breakfast: Scrambled eggs with whole wheat toast and avocado
  • Snack: Cottage cheese with pineapple chunks
  • Lunch: Turkey and avocado wrap with a side of mixed greens
  • Snack: Protein smoothie with banana and almond milk
  • Dinner: Grilled lean steak with mashed sweet potatoes and asparagus

Day 3 - Fiber & Nutrient Boost

  • Breakfast: Whole-grain pancakes with honey and a side of blueberries
  • Snack: Handful of mixed nuts and dark chocolate
  • Lunch: Quinoa and black bean bowl with roasted vegetables
  • Snack: Carrot sticks with hummus
  • Dinner: Baked cod with a spinach and feta salad

Day 4 - Lean Muscle Support

  • Breakfast: Egg white omelet with spinach and feta
  • Snack: Hard-boiled eggs with a sprinkle of salt and pepper
  • Lunch: Grilled shrimp with brown rice and saut√©ed zucchini
  • Snack: Low-fat cheese with whole grain crackers
  • Dinner: Baked tofu with stir-fried bell peppers and cashews

Day 5 - Healthy Fats & Recovery

  • Breakfast: Chia seed pudding with almond milk and raspberries
  • Snack: Handful of almonds and a green smoothie
  • Lunch: Grilled salmon with avocado salad and quinoa
  • Snack: Dark chocolate and pumpkin seeds
  • Dinner: Roasted chicken with mashed cauliflower and steamed green beans

Day 6 - Strength & Endurance

  • Breakfast: Peanut butter on whole-grain toast with banana slices
  • Snack: Protein bar or homemade granola bar
  • Lunch: Tuna salad with mixed greens and balsamic dressing
  • Snack: Celery sticks with almond butter
  • Dinner: Grilled turkey burger with roasted sweet potatoes and coleslaw

Day 7 - Light & Detoxifying

  • Breakfast: Smoothie with spinach, banana, and Greek yogurt
  • Snack: Fresh fruit salad with a drizzle of lemon juice
  • Lunch: Grilled vegetable and hummus wrap
  • Snack: Avocado toast on whole-grain bread
  • Dinner: Baked tilapia with steamed asparagus and wild rice
